## ACT2

wo days later the group met at the asylum as scheduled.

"Is the relay station ready?" Wang asked.

"It's online and running," the staff replied.

"Clara, are you ready?" She gave a thumbs-up as an answer.

Wang checked on the camera mounted on his helmet. "Alright, the camera's ready, and there is no anomaly. We will live on three, two." Wang made a gesture of "one" with his index finger, a signal for Clara to begin the show. 

"Hi, everyone!! It's your favorite diving host, Diving Diva," Clara smiled and waved her hands at the camera mounted at Wang's helmet.

"Today, we will go mesh diving on old Marduk Asylum with our guide, Sven." The camera aimed at Sven. He awkwardly waved his hand. "Greetings," he greeted in a monotone voice.

They ventured into the deeper area of the plant. Sven repeatedly shoots scan marker spells along the way. Ensuring they don't lose their way.

"So, what's the deal with this concrete plant?" Clara asked.

"Good question; nobody knows," Sven answered. 

"It was already here when this dive zone was discovered." Sven shot another scan spell towards the path forward.

"In the past, people used this mesh as a jumping point to the aerial fishery plane of Brentwood. But since we found a safer route, it is mostly abandoned." He casts a marker spell on the ground as they move forward.
